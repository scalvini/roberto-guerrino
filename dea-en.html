Dea

In memoriam
His alter ego. Red hair, lightness, freedom.
00Who Dea was

Dea was his freer, more playful side — another way of being in the world.

Not mere dressing up, but a character with her own name and her own spirit.

01The Christmas night in New York

She was born of a New York night. On Christmas Eve, in the East Village, in a historic venue, he watched Amanda Lepore arrive like a true celebrity.

That encounter was a spark. He decided that, once home, he would bring her to life. On 27 December, in Milan, Dea was born.

02Red hair
"Dea has red hair."
Roberto

He described her so, with a detail and a smile. She was playful and light, and brought into his life a freedom that made him happy.

03An experiment

Dea was also an experiment. Curious about people, Roberto would meet others as Dea, and watch how their gaze changed.

It was an almost anthropological game, as well as a pleasure. A way of being in the world from a different threshold, and of knowing it better.

04An accomplishment

For years he kept her to himself. Then one day he told his analyst, and lived it as a victory.

"Big accomplishment", he wrote. She told him he was right to, that it was a fun, playful and light way to be.

05Dea and the Buddha

Two figures lived in him. Dea, play and lightness. The Buddha, silence and discipline.

They did not contradict each other. They were two roads to the same freedom, and the same peace with himself.
